REFUND POLICY

At Faux Fire Gaming, we value our community and strive to ensure a transparent and fair experience for all our players. Please read our refund policy carefully before making any purchases.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available for digital purchases made within 7 days if the product or service was not used or activated. To be eligible, your request must meet the following criteria:

  • Request submitted within 7 days of purchase
  • Product key not revealed or activated
  • Tournament not yet started

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds under any circumstances:

  • Used or activated game keys
  • Completed tournament entry fees
  • Promotional or discounted bundles
  • Virtual currency or in-game purchases
  • Subscription services after first billing cycle

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us via our Contact Us page and include:

  • Your transaction ID (found in your confirmation email)
  • Reason for refund request
  • Proof of purchase if requested

Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 3–7 business days after approval. You will receive:

  • Email confirmation when request is received
  • Notification of approval/denial within 48 hours
  • Final confirmation when refund is issued

Refunds will be credited to your original payment method. Processing times may vary by financial institution.

Abuse of Refunds

We monitor refund requests to prevent abuse of our policy. Accounts exhibiting patterns of:

  • Excessive refund requests
  • Fraudulent claims
  • Policy circumvention

May be subject to restrictions including but not limited to: temporary suspension, permanent ban, or revocation of refund privileges.
