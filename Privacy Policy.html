PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 2023

At Faux Fire Gaming, we are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your personal data. This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, date of birth (for age verification)
  • Account Information: Username, password (hashed), profile preferences
  • Gameplay Data: Statistics, achievements, match history, and performance metrics
  • Technical Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser data
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through our payment providers (we don't store full card details)

How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us deliver and improve our services:

  • Create and manage your gaming account
  • Provide personalized gaming experiences and recommendations
  • Process transactions and prevent fraud
  • Send service updates, tournament alerts, and promotional communications (you can opt-out)
  • Analyze gameplay to improve game balance and features
  • Ensure compliance with our Terms of Service
  • Respond to customer support inquiries

Data Protection & Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure storage with access controls and regular security audits
  • Regular software updates and vulnerability testing
  • Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Two-factor authentication options for account security

While we implement these measures, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics, and customer support tools
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

We never sell your personal data to third-party advertisers.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Deliver targeted advertisements (you can opt-out)
  • Prevent fraudulent activity

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Lodge complaints with data protection authorities

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

As a global service, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for these transfers.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes through email or in-platform notices. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
